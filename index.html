Trip's in my sister's neighborhood: Williamsburg

Originally I'm from Brooklyn but once my sister moved it became my third home. When I go to my sister's house these are the 3 places that you'd find us at!

Domino Park

Domino Park is located in Williamsburg Brooklyn Ny along the East River at the former site of the Domino Sugar Refinery



Ruby's Cafe

Rubys Cafe is one of my favorite cafes to eat at. The first time I had it I was in Manhattan but my sister found one in Williamsburg at 98 Berry St, Williamsburg, NY 11249



Chip city

Chip City I'd say is almost better than Crumble cookie. The cookies taste better and the quality in my opinion is also better
